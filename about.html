Our Story

Hey! We're Tayvin and Mateo, two students trying to find a way to output our creativity and craving to spend money on clothe sampling. We share a vision: to create versatile, expressive but minimalistic clothing rooted in streetwear. Working your 12 hour shift, the gym, or going to the local gas station at 2 AM? Our clothes are suited for any situation where comfortability but presentability is needed.
